About Us

About Us

We are a family-owned book pickup service with a passion for books, reading, and sustainability. We service the majority of the LA area including the San Fernando Valley and Simi Valley. Your donated books are redistributed into the hands of new readers, all while eliminating the need for you to handle loading and transport. Let us do the heavy lifting!

Greater Los Angeles

  • San Fernando Valley
  • Simi Valley
  • And most of the greater LA area
The Fine Print

We Take Any and All Books

The only exceptions are magazines and books with heavy damage (moldy, torn, broken spine, water damage, heavy wear and tear).

How It Works

How It Works

01

Simply fill out our pickup request form and one of our team members will respond within one business day with a quote and a pickup appointment.

02

You are not required to be present during pickup, just leave the books outside ready for us and we will arrive and take care of the rest! The books can be in boxes, bins, or bags.

What Do We Do With The Books?

Resale

The books we receive are distributed to libraries, thrift stores, and various charity organizations that help make it possible for them to find a new home. A small portion of books are also sold in order to help fund the costs of operating our service, allowing us to continue to be able to re-home books.

We are a small business trying hard to grow. We will find loving homes for your books!
